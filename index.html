About REVO

  • Hey, I’m Ryan Manchanda — BTech CSE student at IIIT Raichur and CEO of Revo Softwares. Revo started as a small idea by my dad, Mr. Rishi Manchanda, who wanted to build a system that reduces the administrative load on schools.

    What began as one simple tool has grown into multiple products — attendance systems, quiz boards, scoreboards, dashboards and more — each built because someone genuinely needed it.

    Back then, we had no team or funding. Just an idea, a laptop, and the drive to create something useful.

    Today, Revo is used across schools and institutions, and we're constantly improving and building smarter tech. The mindset is still the same: clean, fast, reliable systems that make life easier.

    This is just the beginning — the rise of a homegrown tech force from Punjab.
